- **Flipper Zero**: A portable multi-tool for pentesters and geeks in a toy-like body that can hack digital stuff, such as radio protocols, access control systems, hardware, and more.
- **Features**: Flipper Zero has a LCD display, a 5-button directional pad, a microSD card slot, a Bluetooth module, an NFC module, a 125 kHz RFID module, a CC1101 transceiver, an IR transmitter and receiver, a 1-Wire connector, and 13 GPIO pins.
- **Cyber-dolphin**: Flipper Zero has a curious personality of a cyber-dolphin that interacts with digital systems in real life and grows while you use it.
- **Open-source and customizable**: Flipper Zero is fully open-source and can be extended with your own code and hardware. You can also connect to Flipper Zero via USB and Bluetooth to control it from your desktop or smartphone.
