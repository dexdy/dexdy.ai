URL :: https://github.com/monicahq/monica

Monica is an open-source Personal Relationship Management (PRM) system designed to document and organize interactions with friends, family, and business relationships. Key features include contact management, reminders, activity logging, document and photo uploads, and custom definitions for genders and activities. Aimed at individuals wanting to keep track of personal connections, Monica is not a social network or AI assistant. Contributions to its development are welcomed. Monica prioritizes simplicity, transparency, and user data privacy, and is available in multiple languages.
