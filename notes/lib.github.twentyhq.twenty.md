URL :: https://github.com/twentyhq/twenty

The GitHub repository "twentyhq/twenty" is focused on developing a modern, open-source CRM alternative to Salesforce, with community collaboration at its core. The project aims to provide users with flexibility and control over their CRM needs. Key features include managing and tracking customers, creating opportunities, and integrating tasks in a timeline view. The platform emphasizes intuitive navigation with keyboard shortcuts and search features. As a Public Benefits Company, Twenty commits to broader stakeholder impacts and open source community benefits. The project, still in its alpha development phase, encourages community contributions and discussions.
