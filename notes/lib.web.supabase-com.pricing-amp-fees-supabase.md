Supabase offers various pricing plans starting with a Free option, perfect for passion projects, and scales up to Pro, Team, and Enterprise levels for more demanding applications. The Free plan includes 500 MB of database space, 50,000 monthly active users, and more, with projects pausing after 1 week of inactivity. Pro plans begin at $25/month, offering increased limits and additional features like daily backups. Team plans start at $599/month, including everything in Pro plus advanced security and support options. Enterprise plans are customizable for large-scale applications. Additional compute and storage options are available for scaling needs. For detailed information, visit [Supabase Pricing](https://supabase.com/pricing).
