Umami is a simple, fast, privacy-focused alternative to Google Analytics. It is a self-hosted web analytics solution that aims to provide a friendlier, privacy-focused alternative to Google Analytics and a free, open-source alternative to paid solutions. It collects only the metrics you care about and respects the privacy of your users. The Umami software is available on GitHub under the umami-software organization, and its repositories include the main Umami project, the Umami website, and other related components. The latest release of Umami (v2.8.0) includes new features, interface updates, and various fixes.
